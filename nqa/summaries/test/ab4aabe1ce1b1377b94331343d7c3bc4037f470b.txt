Brandon Lang ( McConaughey ) is a former college football star who , after sustaining a career-ending injury , takes a job handicapping football games . His success at choosing winners catches the eye of Walter Abrams ( Pacino ) , the slick head of one of the biggest sports consulting operations in the United States . Walter takes Brandon under his wing , and soon they are making tremendous amounts of money . Lang s in-depth knowledge of the game , leagues and players brings in big winnings and bigger clients .
